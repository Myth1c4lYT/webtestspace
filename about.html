What Is ScreenMaker?

ScreenMaker is a windows app that adds extra customisation to your desktop! You can add widgets, a customisable dock, you can even add widgets from external apps!

How Does ScreenMaker Work?

ScreenMaker works by getting the windows desktop customisation settings and then customises them through the app, ScreenMaker then uses external apps to install widgets and docks to your desktop!
